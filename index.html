Support Services for Formerly Incarcerated Women in NYC

The Women Transcending Resource Guide is dedicated to providing information on services that support formerly incarcerated women who are reentering society in the New York City area.

Some of the listed resources, as well as non-gender-specific resources, can be found in the NYPL's Connections 2021 report.
